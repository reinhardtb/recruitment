# Role Context - Booking.com Fintech Architect

## Position Summary
**Fintech Architect** at Booking.com's FinTech Foundations team - the central nervous system of the entire FinTech Products division.

## Organization Structure

### FinTech Business Unit
- **Global Locations**: Amsterdam, Bangalore, London, Shanghai
- **Mission**: Remove financial friction from travel process through innovation
- **Goal**: Simplify buying/selling travel products for travelers and partners

### FinTech Foundations Team
- **Role**: Central nervous system of FinTech Products division
- **Purpose**: Build critical, horizontal platform and services for product teams
- **Focus**: Complex, cross-cutting challenges (scalability, reliability, core functionality)
- **Impact**: Stable bedrock for future growth, influencing entire FinTech ecosystem

### Dual Alignment
1. **Primary**: FinTech Foundations team (reports to Director)
2. **Secondary**: Member of broader FinTech Products Architecture team
- This ensures driving foundational services while influencing overall product architecture

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Architectural Excellence
- Key role in **FinTech Architecture Review Board**
- Ensure quality, security, scalability standards

### 2. Team Guidance
- Support engineering teams in solution design
- Balance project deadlines with long-term architectural vision

### 3. Theory-Practice Bridge
- Validate designs against real-world implementation
- Use feedback to refine strategies

### 4. Best Practices Champion
- Develop reusable architectural patterns
- Create technical documentation
- Lead knowledge-sharing sessions

### 5. Technology Simplification
- Reduce architectural complexity
- Minimize unnecessary system coupling

### 6. Technical Strategy
- Establish and maintain **FinTech Product's Tech Radar**
- Evaluate emerging technologies
- Drive innovation, guide investment

### 7. Stakeholder Management
- Effective communication management
- Strategic alignment, risk identification, consensus building
